# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Prefer evidence-based technical discussion.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or sustained hostility.
- Deliberate sabotage of project integrity or reproducibility.

## Scope
This applies to project spaces, issue trackers, PR discussions, and related communication channels.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or take other actions to preserve a safe and productive environment.
